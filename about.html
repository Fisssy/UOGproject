Company Overview

Kazaka is one of the leading car rental companies in Singapore

Kazaka Inc was founded in September 2007 and is headquartered in Singapore. As a leading car rental service provider of Singapore, Kazaka actively draws lessons from the successful car rental model in the world and provides specialized car rental services such as short-term rental, long-term rental and financial leasing, as well as complete supporting services such as national rescue and return of vehicles from other places. As of June 2018, Kazaka Inc has more than 1,100 service outlets in more than 300 major cities in China, serving tens of millions of individual customers and tens of thousands of corporate customers. It is now the leading company in the automobile rental industry of Singapore.

With the service concept of 'Any One, Any Time, Any Car, Any Where'.Kazaka is committed to promoting green travel and a new automobile consumption culture and is committed to becoming the preferred automobile rental service brand in Singapore. It is committed to promoting the development of the automobile rental industry and automobile industry in Singapore.

According to a third-party Roland Berg survey, Kazaka is currently Singapore's most recognized car rental service provider, and in the fleet size, network coverage, market share and business income and other operational and financial indicators, in Singapore's car rental industry absolutely leading position.
